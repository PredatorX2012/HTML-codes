Madara Uchiha (Japanese: うちは マダラ, Hepburn: Uchiha Madara) is a manga and anime character in the Naruto series created by Masashi Kishimoto. He appears for the first time in "Part II" of the manga and the Shippuden anime adaptation(war arc), and serves as a major antagonist of the series. He, along with its first Hokage Hashirama Senju, is one of the co-founders of Konohagakure
